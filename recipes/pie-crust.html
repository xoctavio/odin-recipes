Ingredients

  • 11/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, chilled and diced
  • 1/4 cup ice water

Directions

  1. Combine flour and salt in a large bowl. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Mix in water, a tablespoon at a time, until dough comes together. Shape into a disc, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.

  2. Roll dough out to a 12-inch circle on a lightly floured surface. Carefully place crust in a 9-inch pie dish. Press dough evenly into the bottom and sides. Trim excess dough and flute edges. Bake or fill crust as desired.
